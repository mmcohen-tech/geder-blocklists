! NOTES
! ============================================================
! Imgur: heavily used as a generic image host across Reddit, forums,
! tutorials, and blogs. Blocking it will break image display on many
! legitimate pages. If customer complaints come in, consider moving
! imgur.com to a per-household whitelist rather than removing from the
! global list.
!
! Rumble and BitChute are NOT in this list — they're in social.txt because
! they function primarily as YouTube alternatives with creator channels
! and subscription feeds rather than pure shock aggregation.
!
! Family Shield upstream already blocks:
!   - Pornography
!   - Known phishing / malware
!   - Proxy / VPN-bypass services
! So those categories are intentionally NOT duplicated here.

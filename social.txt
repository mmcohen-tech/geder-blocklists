! Title: Geder - Social Media Blocklist
! Description: Major social media platforms + known porn-circumvention sites
!              (Bing, Reddit). Excludes LinkedIn by design.
! Maintained by: Geder
! Last updated: 2026-04-19
! Format: AdGuard / AdBlock syntax. ||domain^ blocks domain + all subdomains.
!
! NOTE ON SCOPE: OpenDNS Family Shield handles core adult content upstream,
! but Bing and Reddit are included here specifically because:
!  - Bing Image Search can display adult content when SafeSearch is disabled,
!    making it a common way around Family Shield.
!  - Reddit hosts a huge volume of adult content on NSFW subreddits, served
!    from reddit.com domains that Family Shield can't distinguish from SFW.
! Blocking both at the DNS level closes those gaps.
!
! CHANGED 2026-04-19: Discord and Telegram moved to dangers.txt (their primary
! risk is predator/grooming, not general social media). If a customer wants
! Discord/Telegram blocked, they should enable Kid Dangers.

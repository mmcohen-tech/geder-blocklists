! Title: Geder - Console Gaming Platforms Blocklist
! Description: Xbox, PlayStation, and Nintendo online service domains.
!              Blocking these disables ALL online functionality on game
!              consoles: multiplayer, updates, digital downloads, online
!              store, parental controls sync, friends lists.
! Maintained by: Geder
! Last updated: 2026-04-19
! Format: AdGuard / AdBlock syntax. ||domain^ blocks domain + all subdomains.
!
! IMPORTANT FOR CUSTOMERS:
! This list is separate from online-games.txt because of its severe impact.
! If you enable this, your consoles will be offline-only. Games that
! require an internet connection (most modern ones) won't work. Most
! families who own consoles do NOT want this list enabled.
!
! Use this list if your household is doing a full console detox, or if
! consoles are prohibited in your home and you want to block any kid
! who brings one from connecting it to your WiFi.

! NOTES

! NOTES
! ============================================================
! Discord and Telegram are deliberately in BOTH social.txt and this list.
! Rationale: a family that wants ONLY kid-safety filtering (not full social
! media blocking) should still have their kids protected from these two
! platforms, which have extensive documented grooming problems.
!
! WhatsApp is NOT included. While it's a messaging app, it generally
! requires exchanging phone numbers — less of a cold-approach predator
! vector than open-DM platforms like Discord or Telegram. Families that
! want WhatsApp blocked can request a custom addition.
!
! Family Shield upstream blocks pornography. This list does NOT duplicate
! that; it focuses on platforms that expose kids to strangers or bullying
! regardless of explicit content.
